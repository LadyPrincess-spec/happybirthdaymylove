Dear Lord Gentle,

Today, you turn twenty-two.

And while birthdays are supposed to be about getting older, I think today is really about celebrating the fact that you exist.

Twenty-two years ago, the world received someone kind, patient, gentle, caring, and full of love.

Maybe nobody knew it then. But I know it now. Because I have been lucky enough to know you.

Thank you for being patient with me. Thank you for understanding me. Thank you for handling me gently. Thank you for being my safe place.

One of my favorite things about you is how naturally caring you are. You make people feel comfortable. You make people feel seen. You make people feel safe. And I think that is one of the rarest gifts a person can have.

I know there are things you're still working toward. The position you hope to achieve. The future you're building. The business dream you keep in your heart.

And I hope this year brings you closer to every single one of them.

I hope your hard work gets rewarded. I hope good opportunities find you. I hope your dreams slowly become plans. And your plans become reality.

Most of all, I hope you never lose your softness. The world often teaches people to become harder. But your gentleness is one of the most beautiful things about you.

And if one day you ever doubt yourself, please borrow my eyes for a moment. Because through my eyes, you are someone worth admiring. Someone worth loving. Someone worth celebrating. Today and every day after.
